On the Keeping of a Sea Journal

Eleanor A. Fairweather · 19 June 2026 · 7 min

Whenever I find myself growing grim about the mouth, I take to the margins of an old ledger and there set down the weather. The practice is older than either of us; sailors kept their reckonings in ink that would not run, on paper that would not rot, and trusted the page to remember.
